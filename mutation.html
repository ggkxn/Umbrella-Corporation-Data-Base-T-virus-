>> I. T-VIRUS MUTAGENIC PROFILES

PROFILE A-01: STANDARD ZOMBIE

[Host]: Human (Homo sapiens)

[Primary Mutation]: Necrosis of epidermal and muscular tissue. Cerebral degradation (neocortex), resulting in loss of higher cognitive function and aggressive behavior.

[Behavioral Traits]: Predatory, driven by instinct to consume organic matter. Slow locomotion, but highly resilient to non-head trauma.

[Vulnerabilities]: Head trauma (critical). Fire. Exposure to daylight (slow degradation).

PROFILE A-02: LICKER (Evolutionary Stage 2)

[Host]: Human (T-Virus exposure with specific genetic markers)

[Primary Mutation]: Extreme muscular hypertrophy, epidermal atrophy revealing muscle tissue. Ocular degeneration compensated by highly sensitive auditory/olfactory senses. Elongated, prehensile tongue.

[Behavioral Traits]: Highly aggressive, agile, capable of wall-crawling. Relies on sound and smell for hunting. Extremely dangerous in close quarters.

[Vulnerabilities]: Loud noises (distraction). High-caliber firearms (rapid neutralization).

>> II. BIO-ORGANIC WEAPON (B.O.W.) CLASSIFICATION

B.O.W. TYPE: TYRANT (T-002 Model)

[Project Codename]: "Tyrant"

[Development Goal]: Create the ultimate living weapon, capable of independent operation and combat superiority.

[Key Characteristics]: Enhanced strength, speed, and resilience. Significant cellular regeneration. Programmed to eliminate specific targets. Exposed cardiac muscle (weak point).

[Status]: CRITICAL. Multiple field deployments. High success rate in target neutralization, but prone to uncontrolled mutation under extreme stress.

B.O.W. TYPE: HUNTER (Gamma Series)

[Project Codename]: "Hunter"

[Development Goal]: Mass-producible close-quarters combat unit, designed for rapid extermination of human targets.

[Key Characteristics]: Genetically engineered chimera (human/reptile). Razor-sharp claws, extreme agility, and predatory intelligence. Highly adaptive to various environments.

[Status]: ACTIVE. Successful deployment in multiple facilities. Cost-effective and highly efficient. Minimal T-Virus mutation risk in controlled environments.
